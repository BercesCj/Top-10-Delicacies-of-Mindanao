Suman sa Lihiya: A Traditional Mindanao Rice Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups glutinous rice
  • 1/4 cup lye water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Banana leaves for wrapping

Steps:

  1. Soak the glutinous rice in water for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain and mix the rice with lye water, sugar, and salt.
  3. Lay out banana leaves, place a spoonful of rice in the center, and wrap tightly.
  4. Steam the wrapped rice for about 30 minutes.
  5. Serve with sugar or coconut milk on the side.

  3. Sweetened Suman sa Lihiya: While some recipes for Suman sa Lihiya are not overly sweet, others add more sugar to the rice mixture, making the suman a sweeter treat that can be enjoyed as a dessert.
  4. Suman sa Lihiya with Coconut Flakes: Some versions of suman include shredded coconut mixed into the rice mixture, which adds a slight texture and coconut flavor directly to the rice cake.
