Sinuglaw: A Flavorful Fusion of Grilled and Ceviche

Ingredients:

  • 200g pork belly (grilled)
  • 300g tuna or any firm fish (cut into cubes)
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 1 cucumber (diced)
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • 2-3 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup vinegar (or more to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Grill the pork belly until fully cooked and slightly crispy. Let it cool and cut it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, combine the raw fish with vinegar, salt, and pepper. Let it marinate for about 10-15 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the grilled pork, marinated fish, onion, cucumber, tomatoes, and chilies.
  4. Toss everything together, adjust the seasoning with more vinegar, salt, or pepper if needed.
  5. Serve fresh and chilled.
